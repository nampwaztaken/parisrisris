Chromebook Unenrollment Method

  1. Remember to backup your data!
  2. Press ESC + REFRESH + POWER
  3. After you see the recovery screen, press CTRL + D then ENTER / SPACE
  4. On the "Welcome to your Chromebook" screen, wait until you see the quick set up with Android button (DO NOT PRESS GET STARTED IF IT DOESN'T SHOW IMMEDIATELY)
  5. Hit CTRL + ALT + SHIFT + R and click "cancel"
  6. Click "Enter your Google account email and password" — it should prompt to connect to a network
  7. Open quick settings from the bottom right and connect to a network and sign in to your PERSONAL Google Account

Post-Unenrollment

After signing in, you can sign out and return to the welcome screen. Proceed through OOBE by clicking "Get Started" and "Next." You’ll be given three options to sign in. Use the same email. When it hangs on the "Please wait" screen, simply restart or press Alt + VolUp + X. You’ll be taken to the lockscreen.

From there, you’re done. You can sign out or reboot.

(NOTE: DO NOT POWERWASH!)
